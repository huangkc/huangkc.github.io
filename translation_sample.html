亞洲銀行家協會 11月台北辦年會

Asian Bankers Association to Hold Annual Meeting in Taipei in November

為因應大數據革命、促進亞太地區銀行業合作發展，包含日本、越南、菲律賓、香港、伊朗與台灣等在內的亞洲銀行家協會（ABA）成員，昨天決議11月將在台北舉辦年會，並舉辦CEO論壇。

To respond to the Big Data revolution and promote cooperation in the banking industry in the Asia Pacific region, the Asian Bankers Association (ABA), whose members include Japan, Vietnam, the Philippines, Hong Kong, Iran, and Taiwan, decided yesterday to hold the annual meeting and CEO forum in Taipei in November.

協會成員昨天來台，於中國信託金融園區召開計畫委員會，會中決定11月以「無國界成長」為主題，設定「亞洲銀行的區域整合」、「跨文化客戶之金融管理」及「數位化：全球科技帶來的商機」等議題，在台北舉行年會討論。

ABA members arrived in Taiwan yesterday to attend the planning committee conference at the CTBC Financial Park. It was decided during the conference that “Growth Without Borders” will be the theme for the November annual conference in Taipei, and discussion topics will include “Regional Consolidation of Asian Banks,” “Financial Management for Cross-cultural Clients,” and “Digitalization: Business Opportunities Brought About by Global Technology.”

台灣是目前ABA擁有最多會員銀行的會員國，新任ABA會長、中信金總經理吳一揆表示，大數據革命正影響全球，金融數位化、網路化、行動化為大勢所 趨，未來亞太地區各銀行如何善用雲端數據資料優化經營策略，進而發現商機，因應新興科技快速發展帶來嚴峻挑戰，希望藉由ABA交流平台，促進亞太地區銀行 業經營的發展與合作。

Taiwan currently has the largest number of member banks among ABA members. New ABA President and CTBC Financial Holdings CEO Daniel Wu pointed out that the Big Data revolution is having a global impact and digitalization, network development, and mobility of finance is an undeniable trend. In the future, banks in the Asia Pacific region must take advantage of cloud data and optimize operating strategies to uncover business opportunities and respond to the formidable challenges due to the rapid development of emerging technologies. It is hoped that the ABA exchange platform can be used to promote development and cooperation in the banking industry in the Asia Pacific region.

根據計畫，11月由中國信託銀行主辦的第32屆ABA年會，將邀請亞太地區知名金融專家、學者針對上述議題發表專題演講，預計至少有250位亞洲各國銀行代表齊聚中國信託金融園區，針對亞太金融環境、業務發展及經營策略，深入剖析研討並切磋經驗。

According to the plan, the 32nd ABA annual conference sponsored by CTBC in November will invite renowned financial experts and academics in the Asia Pacific region to speak on the aforementioned topics. At least 250 banking representatives from Asia Pacific countries are expected gather at the CTBC Financial Park and hold in-depth analysis and discussions and exchange ideas on financial environment, business development, and operating strategies in the region.

从2013年开始，每年全国两会期间，中共中央总书记、国家主席、中央军委主席习近平都会来到解放军代表团，出席代表团全体会议，听取代表发言，并发表重要讲话。

Since 2013, Chinese Communist Party Central Committee General Secretary, President, and Central Military Commission Chairman Xi Jinping has been coming to the People’s Liberation Army delegation to attend the full meeting to the PLA delegation during the period of the two national Conferences to listen to the delegates and give important speeches.

2013年全国两会

　　·谈强军目标：

　　建设一支听党指挥能打胜仗作风优良的人民军队 　　习近平指出，建设一支听党指挥、能打胜仗、作风优良的人民军队，是党在新形势下的强军目标。听党指挥是灵魂，决定军队建设的政治方向；能打胜仗 是核心，反映军队的根本职能和军队建设的根本指向；作风优良是保证，关系军队的性质、宗旨、本色。全军要准确把握这一强军目标，用以统领军队建设、改革和 军事斗争准备，努力把国防和军队建设提高到一个新水平。

2013 National Two Conferences

-On the goal of strengthening the military

To construct an outstanding people’s army that obeys the Party’s command and fights to win: Xi noted that to construct a people’s army that obeys the Party’s command, fights to win, and has proper work style is the goal of strengthening the military under the new situation. Obeying the Party’s command is the soul that determines the political direction of army building; fighting to win is the core that reflects the basic function of the military and the basic direction of army building; proper work style is the assurance that determines the nature, purpose, and essence of the military. Everyone in the military must accurately grasp this goal of military strengthening, use it to lead army building, reform, and preparations for military struggle, and strive to elevate national defense and army building to a new height.

2014年全国两会

　　·谈实现强军目标：

　　勇担历史责任、深化国防和军队改革、军民融合深度发展 　　习近平强调，实现强军目标，必须勇敢承担起我们这一代革命军人的历史责任。面对新的形势任务，必须以只争朝夕的精神推进国防和军队现代化。我们希望和平，但任何时候任何情况下，都决不放弃维护国家正当权益、决不牺牲国家核心利益。

2014 National Two Conferences

-On implementing the goal of strengthening the military

Bravely take on the historic responsibility, deepen national defense and military reform, and further military-civilian integration Xi stressed that in order to achieve the goal of strengthening the military, it is necessary to bravely shoulder the historical responsibility of our generation of revolutionary soldiers. Facing the new situation and mission, we must promote national defense and military modernization with the spirit of seizing the moment. We hope for peace. However, we will not, at any time under any circumstances, fail to defend rightful national rights or sacrifice the core national interest.

2015年全国两会

　　·谈开创强军兴军新局面：

　　把军民融合发展上升为国家战略树立一盘棋思想 　　习近平希望全军认真贯彻党中央、中央军委决策部署，加快推进国防和军队建设，并重点就深入实施军民融合发展战略提出要求。他强调，把军民融合发 展上升为国家战略，是我们长期探索经济建设和国防建设协调发展规律的重大成果，是从国家安全和发展战略全局出发作出的重大决策。

2015 National Two Conferences

-On Creating a New Situation in Strengthening the Military

Elevate military-civilian integration to national strategy and establish the ideology of the organic whole Xi hopes that the entire military will implement the deployment decisions of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee and the Central Military Commission to accelerate national defense development and army building and proposed requirements for focusing on executing the strategy of military-civilian integration. He stressed that elevating military-civilian integration to the level of national strategy is an important achievement of our extended exploration of the development patterns in the coordinated development of economic construction and national defense construction as well as an important decision made from the perspective of the overall situation of national security and national development strategy.
